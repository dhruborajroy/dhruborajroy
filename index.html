Publications & Research

📄 B.Sc. Engineering Thesis (2024 – 2025)

Title: Thermal Footprint of Industrial Development: Assessing Land Surface Temperature and Air Temperature Variations Around the Rampal Power Plant, Bangladesh Using Multi-Temporal Landsat-8 and Geospatial Techniques

Authors: Dhruboraj Roy, Orin Karmaker, Md. Liton Rabbani — Dept. of Civil Engineering, Barishal Engineering College, University of Dhaka.

ORCID: 0009-0007-9447-2154  |  Email: Dhruborajroy.duengg@bec.edu.bd

Conducted a comprehensive spatio-temporal analysis of LST, LULC, NDVI, Air Temperature, and UHI intensity around the 1,320 MW Rampal Maitree Super Thermal Power Project, Bagerhat, using Landsat-8 imagery at biennial intervals (2014–2024).

Key Findings: Max LST rose 10°C (24.30→34.30°C); Bare/Industrial land tripled (9.2%→27.1%); Mean NDVI declined 44% (0.41→0.23); Peak air temperature 40.4°C in 2022; UHI intensity nearly doubled (4.5°C→7.8°C).

Methods: ArcGIS 10.8, ISODATA LULC Classification, Radiative Transfer Equation (LST), AL-Anbari et al. (2019) Air Temperature Regression, Getis-Ord Gi* Hotspot Analysis, Pearson Correlation. Overall Accuracy >80%, Kappa >0.75.
